Catholic School Voucher Program

The Catholic School Voucher Program is a four-year commitment to Catholic education through funding made available by Seeds of Hope Charitable Trust. Two hundred and fifty $3,000 vouchers were available to qualifying families in May of 2006. All 250 vouchers were awarded to student new to the Archdiocese of Denver Catholic Schools within weeks of the announcement.

The 2009-2010 school year is the FOURTH and FINAL year of the Catholic School Voucher Program. All vouchers paperwork is currently being processed by the Office of Catholic Schools and families will be notified, in writing, regarding the status of their voucher renewal application.

Currently, the Office of Catholic Schools and the Seeds of Hope Charitable Trust are no longer accepting Catholic School Voucher Program applications.
